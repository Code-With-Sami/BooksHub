📝 How to Participate?

1️⃣ Essay Writing Competition

✅ Rules & Guidelines:

  • Only registered users can participate.
  • A new topic will be displayed when you access the competition page.
  • A 3-hour countdown timer will start automatically once the page is accessed.
  • If you don’t submit within 3 hours, your entry will be automatically disqualified.

✅ Submission Process:

  1. Read the given topic carefully.
  2. Write your essay (save as a .doc, .docx, or .pdf file).
  3. Click the Upload Submission button.
  4. Submission Confirmation: After uploading, you will receive a confirmation email and a submission status update in your account.

⏳ Live Timer Display:

Time remaining: 03:00:00

2️⃣ Story Writing Competition

✅ Rules & Guidelines:

  • Only registered users can participate.
  • Participants can submit any original short story.
  • Deadline: One week from the competition announcement.

✅ Submission Process:

  1. Write your story (save as a .doc, .docx, or .pdf file).
  2. Click the Upload Submission button.
  3. Submission Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email and can check your submission status in your account.

🏆 Winner Selection & Prizes

  • ✔️ The best essays and stories will be reviewed by a panel of judges.
  • ✔️ Prizes include:
    • 🎁 Popular books (General Knowledge, Grammar, and Storybooks).
    • 📰 Feature in our next journal publication.
  • ✔️ Winners’ names and submissions will be displayed on the home page.
